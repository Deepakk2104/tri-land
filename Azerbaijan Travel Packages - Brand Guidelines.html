01The Idea

Azerbaijan is the Land of Fire — ancient flames, the Caspian shore, snow peaks and Silk-Road cities. Our brand carries one symbol: the buta, the national flame-paisley woven into every carpet. A single, confident mark for the trusted English-first gateway to it all.

/01

Trusted

Verified operators, real reviews, a two-hour reply promise. Every signal says: book with confidence.

/02

Effortless

English-first, mobile-first, WhatsApp-first. Visa guidance and curated itineraries, friction removed.

/03

Aspirational

Honeymoon seafronts, first snow, fire temples. We sell the feeling of a place few have discovered.

02Logo Directions

Three routes explored

Each pairs an Azerbaijani symbol with the wordmark. We took The Buta Flame forward — it is the most ownable, scales to a favicon, and works in a single colour.

Direction A

The Buta Flame

The national paisley read as a living flame, with an inner ember. Organic, warm, unmistakably Azerbaijan.

★ Selected
Direction B

The Summit Mark

An “A” monogram built as a Caucasus peak, crowned with a small flame. Geometric and corporate.

Direction C

The Caspian Seal

A travel-stamp roundel — sun rising over the Caspian. Classic and ornamental, but busy at small sizes.
